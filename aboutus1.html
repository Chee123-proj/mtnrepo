MUND TELECOM is underpinned by some key principles

Electromagnetic waves

These are the waves created as a results of vibrations between an electric field and a magnetic field.
In other words EM waves are
composed of oscillating magnetic and electric fields

Bits, bauds and bandwidth

Bit rate defines the amount of data or information that is transmitted
from one source during a specified time period.Baud is a unit of
transmission speed equal to the number of times a signal changes.
Bandwidth is the maximum rate at which bits can be transferred
from source to a to a destination

Communication channels

are mediums through which you can send a message to its intended
audience. For example, phone calls, text messages, data e.t.c

Analogue and digital signals

Analogue signal is a continuous signal that represents physical
measurements.Digital signals are time seperated signals which
are generated using digital modulation
